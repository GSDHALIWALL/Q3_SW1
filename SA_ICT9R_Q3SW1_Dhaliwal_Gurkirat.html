Money Heist; A Netflix Original


Money Heist Summary

Money Heist, also known as "La Casa de Papel," is a Spanish crime drama television series. The show includes a group of robbers that are led by the Professor who stays behind in his secret room and commands the heist. The heist was carried out in the Royal Mint of Spain. Their goal was to print billions of euros and give them to the people. While the heist is happening, the police and the army are working 24/7 trying to kill or arrest the team.

Professor

The Professor, also known as Sergio Marquina, is the leader or mastermind behind the plan for the money heist. He is a very skilled individual who has planned every detail of the plan, leaving no room for errors. He also recruits the members and commands them accordingly while they carry out the heist.

Berlin

Berlin, also known as Andres De Fonollosa, is one of the members of the team that carried out the heist. Berlin is the brother of Moscow (a team member), and he is referred to as one of the key members of their team. Berlin is known for his leadership skills, as he is the leader of the team inside the Royal Mint of Spain. Though he is the leader of his team, he is still under the command of Professor.

Helsinki

Helsinki, also known as Oksana Aleksandrovna Drachenko, is another member of the team. His task is to provide security to his team and guard the hostages. Due to his size and strength, one of his tasks was to melt the gold of the Royal Mint of Spain.

Nairobi

Nairobi, a mother, also known as Agata Jimenez, is another female member of the team. She was the head of the money printing scheme during the heist of the Royal Mint of Spain. Nairobi develops a relationship with another member of the team, which leads to her death.

Tokyo

Tokyo, also known as Silene Oliveira, is another female member of the team. Though her teammates are experienced criminals, Tokyo was one of the unexperienced criminals when she was recruited. Tokyo also develops a relationship with other members of the team, and as a consequence, she almost dies.
